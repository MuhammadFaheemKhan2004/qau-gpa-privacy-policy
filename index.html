QAU GPA Calculator

Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 1, 2026

1. Introduction

QAU GPA Calculator ("we," "us," "our," or "the App") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and otherwise handle your information when you use our mobile application.

2. Information We Do NOT Collect

The QAU GPA Calculator is designed with privacy in mind. We do NOT collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data, including:

  • Student names
  • Student ID numbers
  • Email addresses
  • Phone numbers
  • Device identifiers
  • Location data
  • Usage analytics
  • Academic records

3. How the App Works

Local Data Storage

  • All course information (course names, marks, credit hours, semesters) is stored locally on your device only
  • This data is never sent to our servers or any third-party services
  • You have complete control over your data and can delete it anytime by clearing the app data

Calculation

  • All GPA calculations (SGPA, CGPA) are performed entirely on your device
  • No calculation data is transmitted anywhere

4. Data You Enter

When you use the QAU GPA Calculator, you voluntarily enter:

  • Course names
  • Marks obtained
  • Credit hours
  • Semester information

This data:

  • Remains on your device only
  • Is not collected, stored, or analyzed by us
  • Is not shared with any third parties
  • Is not used for any purpose other than calculation

5. Third-Party Services

The QAU GPA Calculator does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services such as:

  • Google Analytics
  • Firebase
  • Admob
  • Social media tracking
  • Crash reporting services

6. Permissions

The app requests no permissions from your device. It does not require:

  • Internet access
  • Camera access
  • Microphone access
  • Location access
  • Contacts access
  • Storage access (beyond its own app storage)

7. Security

While all data is stored locally on your device, we recommend:

  • Keeping your device software updated
  • Using device security features (password, biometric lock)
  • Not sharing your device with untrusted users
  • Regularly backing up important academic information

8. Data Deletion

You can delete all your data at any time by:

  1. Opening Settings on your device
  2. Going to Apps > QAU GPA Calculator
  3. Selecting "Storage" > "Clear Data"

Or simply uninstall the application.

9. Children's Privacy

The QAU GPA Calculator is designed for university students aged 18+. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13. If we become aware that a child under 13 has provided us with information, we will take steps to delete such information.

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected by updating the "Last Updated" date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically to stay informed about how we protect your privacy.
